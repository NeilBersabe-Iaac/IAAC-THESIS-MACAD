RE:SETTLE

A Flood Mitigation Strategy
for Urban Resettlements


Revise this in the future(Old Brief)
The growth and decay of the urban morphology in developing countries often are uncontrolled due to a lack of insight and analysis in the decision-making process of planning. The need to accommodate increasing populations and the desire for better living spaces and amenities are challenges that highlight the need for a more performance-based approach to urban development. Multiple stakeholders shape the urban fabric as a system, and the imbalance thereof affects the quality of life of one another. The research will focus on autopoiesis through Game theory as a decision-making model that facilitates the growth and decay of urban elements and analyze the performances of each iteration through machine learning.
